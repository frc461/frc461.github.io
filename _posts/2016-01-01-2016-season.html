Awards:

Walker Warren District Event - District Engineering Inspiration Award

Summary:

The 17th season proved to be an exhilarating journey for our team as we embraced the challenges of FIRST Stronghold with fervor. Dubbed "Rowdy 17," we made a formidable impression at the Walker-Warren District, clinching the titles of Finalist and Engineering Inspiration. These accolades not only celebrated our technical prowess but also underscored our commitment to inspiring the next generation of innovators.

The pinnacle of our season was our qualification for the esteemed FIRST Championship, an achievement we greeted with immense pride and excitement. Venturing to St. Louis, we relished the opportunity to interact with diverse teams from across the globe, forging connections and fostering camaraderie amidst the competitive atmosphere. Our time at the Championship was marked by unforgettable experiences and invaluable learning opportunities, reaffirming our passion for robotics and our dedication to pushing the boundaries of innovation.
