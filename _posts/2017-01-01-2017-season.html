Our 18th season we got to compete in FIRST Steamworks! Rowdy 18 charged full steam ahead as we were the 3rd seeded alliance captain and won the Gracious Professionalism Award at the Tippecanoe District. We also were the 8th seeded alliance captain at the St. Joseph District. The season culminated with our qualification for state.
