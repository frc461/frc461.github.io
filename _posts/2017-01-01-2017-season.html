Awards:

Tippecanoe District Event - Gracious Professionalism Award

Summary:

In our 18th season, we embraced the thrilling challenges of FIRST Steamworks with enthusiasm and determination. Branded as "Rowdy 18," our team surged forward with full steam ahead, leaving a lasting impact on the competition circuit. At the Tippecanoe District, we distinguished ourselves as the 3rd seeded alliance captain, showcasing our strategic acumen and teamwork. Additionally, we were honored to receive the esteemed Gracious Professionalism Award, a testament to our commitment to fostering a spirit of cooperation and mutual respect within the FIRST community.

Our success continued at the St. Joseph District, where we assumed the role of the 8th seeded alliance captain, further solidifying our reputation as formidable competitors. The culmination of our season came with our qualification for the state championship, a testament to our perseverance and dedication throughout the year. This achievement stands as a testament to the hard work and dedication of our team members, mentors, and supporters who have continually pushed the boundaries of innovation and excellence.
