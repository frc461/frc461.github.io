Unified Robotics

This program is hosted with Purdue’s annual program with their Engineering Department. This team is extra special as it creates a safe environment for special needs kids to ask questions and figure things out with mentoring behind them. The goal of this program is to expose them to STEM and break down the barriers for them to be able to learn.
